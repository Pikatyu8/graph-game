Dijkstra Trainer: Mental Shortest Path

How to Play:
  • Choose difficulty and graph mode, then click «New Game» (or press N).
  • Your task is to find the cheapest path from the green start node A to the red end node.
  • Click on nodes/edges or press letters on your keyboard (e.g., A, B, C...) to build your path. Re-clicking a node or pressing its key backtracks your path.
  • After reaching the final node, click «Verify» (or press Enter).
